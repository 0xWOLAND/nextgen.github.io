ABOUT

In the last 50 years since the dawn of the era of information, innovators have continued to perfect the design of modern computers.

Not too long ago, computers were the size of an entire room and could barely add two numbers together. Not only have new developments in technology saved time, they have also been able to save lives. People might wonder, “Where do we go from here?” Just as our modern computers can do hundreds of millions of times more operations than the older server rooms and bulky wires, computers in the next few decades will be able to transform the world into a futuristic utopia.

The NextGen team has decided to explore the idea of a new future for technology, and for the next innovations in computing.

As time passes and more people join the computer and technology industries, there will be drastic changes in the way we interact with the environment, the way we get work done, the ways in which we can manipulate a computer program, the ways in which we can be entertained by video games, and the ways in which we can make sure everyone in the world will be able to participate in these activities with us.
