REFLECTIONS

I am proud of myself for learning about designing layouts and color schemes. Prior to Ezine, I often had trouble telling if something “looked right” from a design point of view. I can see myself using this newfound knowledge in website design, which I often struggled with. Throughout the process of writing the feature article, I was super proud of myself for doing the interviews. Despite my parents having connections with a lot of people that work with artificial intelligence, I went out and searched for interviews on my own volition. Interviewing people that I had no prior connection with helped me become more comfortable with going out into the real world and approaching someone that you don’t know. This skill will be huge for me in my future. I believe that having the right mindset before approaching incredible and decorated A.I. practitioners helped me overcome the initial shock of leaving your comfort zone. Despite the fact that this was the part of writing the feature article that I was the most proud of, it was equally the most difficult part. I am still just a freshman, so going out and finding interviews was incredibly difficult for me. I enjoyed using Photoshop the most out of the three softwares we used in class because it allowed me to turn ugly raster images into beautiful designs. I’ll definitely use these softwares to make graphics for websites that I code. I believe that the main suggestion that I have for the future is learning how to make designs from scratch. Although we did design a few graphics in class, they were off of a pre-constructed template. I believe that not designing these graphics from scratch somewhat hindered my ability to create original designs.
